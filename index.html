Meet Favy

Hi, I'm Favy — your personal hairstylist

With 4+ years of experience transforming hair, I founded Favyhairs to offer intimate, high-end hair services in a warm, private studio setting.

No crowded salon. Just you, me, and beautiful results. Whether you want a fresh cut, stunning braids, luxurious extensions, or a complete color transformation — I’ve got you.

✨ Specialties: Braids, Protective Styling, Natural Hair Care, Nails, Extensions & Lashes

Signature Services

Eyelash extensions

Custom lash mapping with premium silk or mink lashes. Natural to dramatic volume, enhancing eyes for up to 4 weeks. Perfect for a fluttery, effortless look.

Acrylic / Gel nails

Flawless sculpted nails with durable gel or acrylic. Includes cuticle care, custom shapes, and trendy finishes — from minimalist to bold art.

Wig Extensions Installation

Seamless lace front & full lace wig installation. Customized fit, bleaching knots, and styling for a natural hairline. Protective and glamorous.

Braids & Twists

Expert knotless, box braids, Senegalese twists, and more. Scalp-friendly tension, premium braiding hair, and long-lasting styles that turn heads.

Braided wigs

Custom handmade braided wigs (knotless, crochet, or stitch). Convenient and protective, giving you a flawless braided look without the commitment.

Frontal installation

Lace frontal / closure installs with melt technology. Natural parting, baby hairs, and seamless blend with your natural hair.

Wig revamping

Breathe new life into old wigs: detangling, deep conditioning, re-bleaching knots, plucking, and restyling to look brand new.

Wash & Blowout

Luxurious cleanse with premium products, scalp massage, and professional blow-dry. Silky, voluminous results perfect for any occasion.
